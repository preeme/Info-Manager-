Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At K/Polegoda M.V. Media Unit, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and ensuring that the personal information of students, parents, and staff is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using this application, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.


2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Student name, age, address, class, contact numbers, and WhatsApp numbers.
  • Usage Data: Information related to how you interact with the application (e.g., IP addresses, device information, and logs).


3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To register and manage student details.
  • To maintain and update student records.
  • To generate reports, including Excel and PDF exports.
  • To improve the performance and usability of the application.


4. Data Storage and Security

We store student information locally on your device's browser using localStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers. We employ reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.


5. Sharing of Information

We do not share personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law.
  • When explicitly authorized by the school administration.


6. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the student data stored on the application.
  • Update or correct any incorrect information.
  • Delete student records from the application.


7. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies or other tracking technologies.


8. Third-Party Libraries

The application uses third-party libraries, such as:

  • XLSX for Excel export.
  • jsPDF for PDF generation.

These libraries do not access your personal data but are used to improve the functionality of the application.


9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will include a "Last Updated" date.
